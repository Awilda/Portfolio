About Me

My name is Awilda and I come from a background in creative arts. I've worked in theatre, studied fashion design and I'm a diy type of person... Somehow all of that has led me to pursue a career as a web developer.

This path is something I never dreamed of (I thought I would be an actor by now). But I'm loving every minute of my journey- even the challenging ones... Overcoming obstacles is one way to learn and writing code has many.

I am grateful to have found this new passion and I can't wait to share my skills with all my future teams.
